\roadmapSection{Flag}{NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES}{Read Every Morning}

{\small\itshape\color{arligray} These rules apply every single day. Read them before starting.}

\vspace{0.2cm}
\checkitem{Rule 1 --- Document or it didn't happen. Photo before and after every repair. Every circuit, every bug.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 2 --- Read the datasheet before touching any IC. First 10 pages. Every single time.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 3 --- Never practice on a customer board. Buy dead boards. 30+ Android board-level before iPhone.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 4 --- ESD is always real. Anti-static mat + wrist strap before touching any board with ICs.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 5 --- Write ARLIZ the same day. Not the weekend. Context fades in 48 hours. One paragraph tonight.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 6 --- Buy tools phase by phase. Not all at once. Most early-bought tools end up unused.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 7 --- Never buy clone repair boxes (UFI, EFT, Octoplus clones). They destroy customer eMMC permanently.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 8 --- Think before AI. Write your hypothesis first. If you can find it in a datasheet, find it there.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 9 --- Sleep minimum 6 hours. Under 6 hours $\to$ retention drops, problem-solving degrades.}{}
\checkitem{Rule 10 --- Repair income funds everything. Never let income track slip for hobby tracks.}{}
