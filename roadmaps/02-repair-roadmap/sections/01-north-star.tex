\begin{northstarbox}
\textbf{\color{arlizblue}\large $\bigstar$\enspace REPAIR NORTH STAR}

\vspace{6pt}
Become a \textbf{board-level repair specialist} who diagnoses and fixes any mobile, laptop,
or PC at component level --- not just screen swaps and OS reinstalls.
Board-level repair is the skill that separates a \$15/job technician from a \$200/job specialist.
This roadmap takes you from first soldering iron to oscilloscope-guided fault isolation.

\vspace{8pt}
\begin{tabular}{@{\hspace{4pt}}ll}
  \textbf{\color{arlizblue}$\to$} & Weeks 1--8\phantom{0} :\enspace Foundations, soldering, advanced test instruments \\
  \textbf{\color{arlizblue}$\to$} & Weeks 9--22  :\enspace Mobile repair --- Android and iPhone board-level \\
  \textbf{\color{arlizblue}$\to$} & Weeks 23--28 :\enspace Laptop repair --- mechanical to BIOS chip \\
  \textbf{\color{arlizblue}$\to$} & Weeks 29--32 :\enspace PC, Desktop, Monitor, Peripherals \\
  \textbf{\color{arlizblue}$\to$} & Ongoing\phantom{000} :\enspace Advanced diagnostics --- scope, thermal cam, power sequencing \\
\end{tabular}

\vspace{8pt}
\textbf{\color{arlizgold}First income target: Week 12.}
Board-level phone repair $\to$ laptop $\to$ PC. Each track funds the next.
\end{northstarbox}
